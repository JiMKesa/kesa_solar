# kesa_solar
Kesa Solar Parts Mod

This mod contains variours solar panels for probes, rockets, ...<br>
Differents types : flat, curved, deployable, shielded,...<br>

FSP-XS1 : Flat solar panel<br>
CSP-XS1 : Curved solar panel<br>
CSP-SM1 : Curved solar panel<br>
CSP-MD1 : Curved solar panel<br>
CSP-XL1 : Curved solar panel<br>
DSPF-S1 : Deployable flat panel<br>
DSPC-S1 : Deployable Curved Solar Panel<br>
DSPC-M1 : Deployable Curved Solar Panel<br>
DSPF-SAW1 : Deployable Station Solar Panel<br>
DSPF-J1 : Flat Deployable Solar Panel x5<br>
DSPF-S2 : Flat Deployable Solar Panel x2<br>
DFSP-M25 :  Flat Deployable Solar Panel x25<br>
DFSSP-M25 :  Flat Shielded Deployable Solar Panel x25<br>
DSPF-L12 :  Flat Deployable Solar Panel 4x3<br>
